## Scoring Methodology

Each dog receives a **FinalScore** computed from up to 10 weighted factors that adapt to race distance:

| Factor | Sprint (<400 m) | Middle (400–500 m) | Long (>500 m) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Early Speed Index | 30% | 25% | 20% |
| Speed (km/h) | 20% | 20% | 15% |
| Consistency Index (wins/starts) | 10% | 15% | 20% |
| Finish Consistency (std dev) | 5% | 5% | 10% |
| Prize Money | 10% | 10% | 10% |
| Recent Form Boost (≤10 days) | 10% | 10% | 10% |
| Box Bias | 10% | 5% | 5% |
| Trainer Strike Rate | 5% | 5% | 5% |
| Distance Suitability | 5% | 5% | 5% |
| Track Condition Adj. | 5% | 5% | 5% |

**Confidence Tiers** (trifecta confidence):
- **Tier 1**: Top score > 42 and separation > 3 — HIGH confidence, BET
- **Tier 2**: Top score > 40 and separation > 2 — GOOD confidence, BET
- **Tier 3**: Top score > 38 and separation > 1.5 — MODERATE confidence, caution
- **Tier 4**: Below thresholds — LOW confidence, avoid
